About Us

Star Fashion Cleaners was founded in Stittsville Ontario 1989. We pride ourselves in quality service and environmentally friendly cleaning supplies.
We strive to have a seamstress on site at every location. Also if you are not satisfied with the clean of your clothes, we reclean them once more for no charge.
We continually strive to exceed customer expectation by providing friendly service and great values.
